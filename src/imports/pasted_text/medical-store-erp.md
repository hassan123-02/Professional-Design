Design a premium, enterprise-grade Medical Store ERP System (Pharmacy Management ERP) with modern SaaS UI/UX. The design should be suitable for pharmacies, medical stores, drug distributors, healthcare retailers, and multi-branch pharmacy chains. Create a complete ERP ecosystem that combines Inventory Management, POS Billing, Pharmacy Operations, Financial Management, Reporting, and Business Intelligence into one centralized platform.

Design Style:

* Modern enterprise SaaS dashboard
* Premium dark theme with blue, green, and white accents
* Professional healthcare-inspired UI
* Clean typography and spacing
* Glassmorphism where appropriate
* Responsive desktop-first design
* Mobile and tablet compatibility
* Beautiful analytics cards
* Advanced data tables
* Interactive charts and graphs
* Professional forms and modals
* Modern iconography
* Enterprise-level user experience comparable to Odoo, Oracle NetSuite, SAP Business One, Zoho Inventory, and Pharmacy POS systems
